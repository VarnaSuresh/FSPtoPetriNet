Alphabet Pane

The alphabet pane is not displayed by default. It is displayed using the Window menu.

The alphabet consists of two areas, a list of the consituent processes of the composite target on the left and an initially blank text area on the right. The alphabet for a process is displayed by clicking on its name in the list.

The alphabet is at first displayed in the most compact form. Sets and ranges can be expanded/contracted using the vertical toolbar.
